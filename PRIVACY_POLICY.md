## 14. Specific Privacy Practices

### 14.1 Unified Inbox Data Handling

**Message Syncing:**
- Messages synced from connected platforms are stored securely
- Messages are encrypted in transit and at rest
- We do not read message content except for AI categorization
- You can disconnect platforms at any time

**AI Processing:**
- Messages are processed by AI (OpenAI, Claude) for categorization
- AI processing is automated; humans do not review content
- AI providers are bound by data protection agreements
- You can opt out of AI features in settings

**Message Deletion:**
- Deleting a message in TATU does not delete it from the original platform
- Disconnecting a platform stops future syncing but doesn't auto-delete past messages
- To delete synced messages: Go to Settings > Connected Platforms > Delete Messages

### 14.2 Video Consultation Privacy

**Live Video:**
- Video calls use WebRTC (peer-to-peer when possible)
- Some data routed through our servers for connectivity
- Video data is not stored during live calls

**Recordings (PRO/STUDIO Only):**
- Artists must notify Clients before recording
- Recordings stored encrypted for 90 days
- Recordings accessible only to the Artist
- Artists can delete recordings anytime

**Best Practices:**
- Always obtain consent before recording
- Do not share recordings publicly
- Use recordings for reference only

### 14.3 Payment Data

**What We Collect:**
- Subscription tier and status
- Payment history (dates, amounts)
- Billing address

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- Full credit card numbers (Stripe handles this)
- CVV codes
- Bank account information

**Stripe Integration:**
- Stripe processes all credit card payments
- Stripe stores full payment information (PCI-compliant)
- We receive limited data from Stripe (last 4 digits, card brand, expiration)

### 14.4 Portfolio Image Metadata

**EXIF Data:**
- We strip EXIF metadata (GPS, camera info) from uploaded images
- This protects Artist privacy and reduces file size

**Face Recognition:**
- We do NOT use facial recognition technology
- We do NOT automatically identify individuals in photos

### 14.5 Location Data

**Artist Location:**
- You choose what location to display (city/state level recommended)
- Precise location (GPS coordinates) never publicly displayed

**Client Location:**
- Used only for search results (finding nearby artists)
- Not stored permanently
- Not shared with Artists unless you choose to

### 14.6 Analytics and Insights

**What We Track:**
- Page views, clicks, time on page
- Search queries (anonymized after 90 days)
- Feature usage
- Conversion funnel metrics

**How We Use It:**
- Improve the Platform
- Identify bugs and issues
- Understand user behavior patterns

**Aggregated Data:**
- We may publish aggregated, anonymized statistics (e.g., "50% of users search for 'realism'")
- Individual users are never identifiable
