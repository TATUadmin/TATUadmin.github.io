## ğŸ¯ Strategic Rationale

### Why 50 Images (Not Unlimited)?

1. **Feels generous** - Most artists never hit 50
2. **Preserves upgrade path** - Very active artists will eventually hit it
3. **Manageable costs** - Storage is cheap but not free
4. **Marketing psychology** - "Up to 50 images" sounds abundant

### Why Video Consultations on FREE?

1. **Client experience** - Clients should be able to video chat with ANY artist
2. **Competitive advantage** - Most platforms don't offer video at all
3. **Artist adoption** - Removes friction to join platform
4. **Natural upgrade trigger** - Active artists will want unlimited calls
5. **Platform lock-in** - Using TATU for consultations = less circumvention

### Why No Time Limits?

1. **Realistic consultations** - Tattoos need 30-60 minutes to discuss properly
2. **Better UX** - No awkward "running out of time" moments
3. **Natural self-regulation** - Artists won't waste their own time (they're busy!)
4. **Technical simplicity** - No timer logic to build/maintain
5. **Trust building** - Shows we're not "nickel and diming"
6. **Cost is minimal** - Most calls will be 20-40 minutes anyway (~$3-4K/year)
