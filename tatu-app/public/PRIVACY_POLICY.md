## 14. Specific Privacy Practices

### 14.1 Unified Inbox Data Handling

**Message Syncing:**
- Messages synced from connected platforms are stored securely on our servers
- Messages are encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- We do not manually read message content; processing is automated for AI categorization only
- You can disconnect platforms at any time
- Syncing frequency: Real-time (webhooks) or every 5-15 minutes (polling)

**AI Processing:**
- Messages are processed by AI (OpenAI GPT-4) for:
  - Categorization (booking request, pricing inquiry, cancellation, general question)
  - Smart reply suggestions
  - Client intelligence (extracting preferences, budget, timing)
  - Sentiment analysis
- AI processing is fully automated; humans do not review content
- AI providers (OpenAI) are bound by strict data protection agreements
- Message content is not used to train third-party AI models
- You can opt out of AI features in Settings > Privacy > Disable AI Processing

**Message Deletion:**
- Deleting a message in TATU does not delete it from the original platform (Instagram, Gmail, etc.)
- Disconnecting a platform stops future syncing but doesn't auto-delete past messages
- To delete synced messages: Go to Settings > Connected Platforms > [Platform Name] > Delete All Messages
- Deleted messages are permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

### 14.1.1 Unified Calendar Data Handling

**Calendar Syncing:**
- Calendar events synced from connected platforms are stored securely on our servers
- Events are encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- Syncing frequency:
  - FREE Tier: Every 60 minutes
  - PRO Tier: Every 15 minutes
  - STUDIO Tier: Every 5 minutes + real-time webhooks (when supported)
- You can manually trigger a sync at any time
- You can disconnect calendars at any time

**Calendar Data We Collect:**
- Event title, description, location
- Start time, end time, duration
- Attendee names and email addresses
- Event status (confirmed, tentative, cancelled)
- Recurring event patterns
- Reminders and notifications
- Calendar name and color
- Time zone information

**Conflict Detection:**
- We analyze your connected calendars to identify overlapping appointments
- Conflict warnings appear when booking new appointments
- Historical conflict data retained for 90 days for analytics
- Conflict detection algorithm is automated; no human review

**AI Email Parsing (PRO/STUDIO):**
- AI scans your connected email accounts for booking confirmations
- Extracts appointment details: date, time, client name, service, location
- Creates calendar events automatically from email confirmations
- Supported platforms: Square, Calendly, Acuity, Booksy, Vagaro, and generic confirmation emails
- You can disable email parsing in Settings > Calendar > Email Parsing

**Two-Way Calendar Sync (STUDIO Only):**
- Changes made in TATU sync back to external calendars (Google, Outlook, etc.)
- Events created in TATU appear in connected external calendars
- Edits, cancellations, and reschedules sync bidirectionally
- Sync conflicts resolved using "last write wins" or manual resolution

**Calendar Disconnection:**
- Disconnecting a calendar stops future syncing
- Historical event data retained for 30 days, then deleted
- Events remain in the original external calendar (not deleted)

### 14.2 Video Consultation Privacy

**Live Video:**
- Video calls use WebRTC (peer-to-peer when possible)
- Some data routed through our servers for connectivity (TURN/STUN servers)
- Video data is NOT stored during live calls (unless recording is enabled)
- Video quality varies by tier: 480p (FREE), 720p (PRO), 1080p (STUDIO)
- No time limits on call duration for any tier

**Recordings (PRO/STUDIO Only):**
- Artists MUST notify Clients before recording and obtain explicit consent
- Recordings stored encrypted (AES-256) for 90 days by default
- Artists can delete recordings anytime (Settings > Video Consultations > Recordings)
- Recordings accessible ONLY to the Artist (not to TATU staff or Clients)
- Recordings are NOT used for AI training or any other purpose

**Transcripts (PRO/STUDIO Only):**
- Video consultations can be automatically transcribed using AI (Deepgram/AssemblyAI)
- Transcripts include speaker identification, timestamps, and searchable text
- Transcripts stored for 12 months
- Artists can download, edit, or delete transcripts anytime
- Transcripts are NOT shared with third parties

**Best Practices:**
- Always obtain explicit verbal consent before recording
- Inform Clients that recordings are for reference only
- Do not share recordings or transcripts publicly without Client consent
- Use recordings to review design discussions and client preferences

### 14.3 Payment Data

**What We Collect:**
- Subscription tier and status
- Payment history (dates, amounts)
- Billing address

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- Full credit card numbers (Stripe handles this)
- CVV codes
- Bank account information

**Stripe Integration:**
- Stripe processes all credit card payments
- Stripe stores full payment information (PCI-compliant)
- We receive limited data from Stripe (last 4 digits, card brand, expiration)

### 14.4 Portfolio Image Metadata

**EXIF Data:**
- We strip EXIF metadata (GPS, camera info) from uploaded images
- This protects Artist privacy and reduces file size

**Face Recognition:**
- We do NOT use facial recognition technology
- We do NOT automatically identify individuals in photos

### 14.5 Location Data

**Artist Location:**
- You choose what location to display (city/state level recommended)
- Precise location (GPS coordinates) never publicly displayed

**Client Location:**
- Used only for search results (finding nearby artists)
- Not stored permanently

**What We Track:**

## 18. Automated Decision-Making and AI Transparency

We use AI for automated decision-making in limited circumstances:

**AI-Powered Features:**
- **Message categorization** (booking request, pricing inquiry, cancellation, general question, urgent)
- **Smart reply suggestions** (contextual response recommendations)
- **Email appointment parsing** (extracting booking details from confirmation emails)
- **Smart scheduling assistant** (STUDIO tier - suggests optimal appointment times)
- **Search result ranking** (relevance scoring for Artist discovery)
- **Spam/abuse detection** (identifying harassment or policy violations)
- **Conflict detection** (identifying overlapping calendar events)
- **Client intelligence** (extracting preferences, budget, timeline from messages)

**How AI Works:**
- We primarily use OpenAI GPT-4 for natural language processing
- AI models are trained on general datasets (not your specific data)
- Your messages and calendar data are processed but NOT used to train third-party models
- AI operates with human oversight for critical decisions (account bans, payment disputes)

**Your Rights:**
- You can opt out of AI features in Settings > Privacy > AI Preferences
- Opting out disables: message categorization, smart replies, email parsing, scheduling suggestions
- Opting out does NOT disable: search functionality, conflict detection (required for safety)
- You can request human review of automated decisions affecting your account
- Contact privacy@tatufortattoos.com for AI-related concerns

**We do NOT use AI for:**
- Credit decisions or loan approvals
- Employment decisions
- Discriminatory practices or profiling
- Automated account termination (human review always involved)

**AI Accuracy:**
- Message categorization: ~85-90% accuracy
- Email appointment parsing: ~80-85% accuracy
- Smart replies: Suggestions only (you always review before sending)
- Conflict detection: ~95%+ accuracy (may have false positives; always verify)
